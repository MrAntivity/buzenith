Presence

Zenith shows up across campus and beyond, building friendships that extend into service and shared causes. Our presence reflects a commitment to community building, partnership, and meaningful impact.

Events & Impact

From philanthropy to mixers, we prioritize visibility, connection, and service.

aKDPhi Mr. Pink

Talent showcase for breast cancer awareness

President Will Sohn performed in the aKDPhi Mr. Pink showcase, dancing to spotlight breast cancer awareness and support fundraising efforts.

Making Strides of Boston • 10/4/2025

Community walk and fundraising milestone

Zenith members joined aKDPhi at the Making Strides of Boston walk. Together, the group raised a combined total of $1,400 for breast cancer research and support services.

Chinatown CleanUp

Service with CSA

Members partnered with CSA to clean Chinatown, supporting local businesses and helping maintain a vibrant community space.

Tree Planting • Tree Eastie

Environmental stewardship in East Boston

Zenith volunteers planted trees with Tree Eastie, learning about tree care and the role of green spaces in neighborhood wellness.

NEU Kappa Phi Lambda x Zenith Mixer

Intercollegiate mixer

A lively mixer with Northeastern KPL featuring games, conversation, and new bonds built across campuses.

NEU Sigmas x Zenith Hangout

Community connection night

Board games and casual conversations made for a relaxed hangout with NEU Sigmas, strengthening inter-organizational friendships.
