Zenith at Boston University

Zenith is an Asian-interest social organization at Boston University, open to all who are curious about culture, connection, and community. We host social events, charity initiatives, and mixers, while collaborating with campus partners at BU and with peers at other schools. Founded in Spring Semester 2025 by Will Sohn and Andy Yoong, Zenith is focused on elevating togetherness with intent and impact.

Vision

Zenith is growing into a signature presence at BU, cultivating a community that feels both close-knit and expansive. We aim to expand collaborations, host larger-scale socials and philanthropy initiatives, and deepen connections across campuses. Over time, our long-term goal is to charter into a fraternity while preserving the values that define us.

Next chapter: toward fraternity chartering + expanded signature events.
